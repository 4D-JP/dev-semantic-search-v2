FORMAT per slide:
- One heading (short, noun-phrase style: "Haftungsrisiken", "Points clés du contrat")

- Reference realistic deal structures, regulatory bodies (BaFin, AMF, FINMA),

- Mix of summary slides (high level) and detail slides (clause-level analysis)

  would actually put in a client presentation.

Do not include a title slide or footer text. Start with the first content slide.

PASSAGE LENGTH AND CONTEXT:
Generate a passage of approximately 800–1000 tokens. The passage is an
excerpt taken from the middle of a longer document — not the opening and
not the conclusion.

Do not include:
- Document title, header, recitals, or preamble

Generate 4–6 consecutive slides from the middle of a deck. Slides

STRUCTURED OUTPUT:
Produce multiple distinct excerpts per call. Return a JSON array where
